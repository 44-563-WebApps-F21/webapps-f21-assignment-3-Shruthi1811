Iron man

Iron Man is a superhero appearing in American comic books published by Marvel Comics. The character was co-created by writer andeditor Stan Lee, developed by scripter Larry Lieber, and designed by artists Don Heck and Jack Kirby. Iron Man is a fictional superhero who wears a suit of armor. It's a special suit that gives Stark superhuman strength, the ability to fly and powerful weapons. But the access is limited to fly and help with the powers.
