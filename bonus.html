The White Baron Zemo

Baron Zemo is the name of several fictional supervillains appearing in American comic books published by Marvel Comics. The two central characters who have used the Baron Zemo title are Heinrich Zemo and Helmut Zemo. His superpowers are Scientific genius; Master swordsman and hand-to-hand combatant; Skilled strategist.
